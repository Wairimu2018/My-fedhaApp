About Us

loanApp Sacco was registered on March 19, 2018.
We aim to positively transform our members lives by offering them an opportunity to save securely and earn returns at attractive interest rates.

Our Mission

To provide affordable financial services to our members to empower them to accomplish their financial goals.

Our Vision

To be the best-managed Cooperative Savings and Credit Society in Kenya.

Our Objectives

Our main objective is to serve our clients who have the desire to improve themselves, their businesses and/or build assets that give them economic power. The Sacco promotes a culture of saving by providing members with an opportunity to accumulate deposits from which loans are made available at attractive rates of interest.
